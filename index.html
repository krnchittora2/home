About Me

Born in the vibrant city of Kota, my educational journey unfolded at the Dakshana Foundation, paving the way for further academic pursuits at the renowned IIT Bombay. These foundational experiences in diverse educational settings have not only equipped me with a robust technological skill set but have also cultivated a spirit of relentless curiosity and innovation.

Navigating through the intricacies of full-stack development, exploring the dynamic landscape of data analytics, and delving into the boundless possibilities of machine learning, my professional journey has been a rich tapestry of experiences. Each step in this odyssey has contributed to the multifaceted professional I am today, shaped by challenges, triumphs, and an unwavering commitment to continuous growth.

In the dynamic realm of consulting and management, I have honed my skills in strategic thinking, problem-solving, and leadership, navigating challenges in fast-paced environments. Beyond the lines of code, I find solace in the harmonies of music and the strategic thrill of games, offering a well-rounded perspective to my pursuits.

This platform is a digital canvas where I share not just my professional achievements but also the anecdotes, challenges, and victories that have defined my journey.
